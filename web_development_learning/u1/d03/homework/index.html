Healthcare industry in US is facing big challenges where the cost of care is increasing at a rate much higher than the quality of care. Opportunities to improve the system have become a focus in recent times. It's my goal to lend my skill-sets towards some of these improvements.

Law

The legal industry has been one of the slowest in adapting technology to improve various processes. My background in law allows me help companies and law firms set up processes to minimize their exposure and reduce cost.
